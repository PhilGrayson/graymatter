Why use an IT Contractor?

Looking for professional help with a project? IT contracting can provide the perfect solution

IT Contractors provide skills to clients on a business-to-business basis, and can often start work within days.

Contractors are hired for a fixed amount of time for a project. For the client, this is an attractive way of budgeting a project as personnnel costs are fixed at the start. This helps to keep a project on time and within budget.

IT Contractors come with a wealth of knowledge and experience. This gives freelance consultants more precise and in-depth knowledge of their field from a global perspective.

Contractors are then able to bring this knowledge to their next opportunity benefiting the project team they are working in.

IT Contractors are easier to lay off subject to the terms of the contract should the project be cancelled or paused.

Requirements Analysis is the process of sitting down and thinking carefully about what your system should be able to do. Starting with looking at your business requirements, this essential first step of computer systems buying process lays the foundation for looking at the systems requirements, and then of course, determining the characteristics of the hardware that will fulfil these needs.

Software

Choosing or designing a software system to suit your needs can be confusing to say the least. Not to mention keeping up with all the latest updates plus all the different versions of commercial software available and all their associated patches and hotfixes, then of course there is the complexities of installing the required applications and tailoring them to your needs.

A Software Requirements Specification (SRS) is a comprehensive description of the intended purpose of a software system and how it is expected to perform, it lays out the foundation for the development of a new product and for the types of user documentation and media that will be required later in the project development life cycle.
